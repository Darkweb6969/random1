WE BUILD BAGS
FOR REAL JOURNEYS

ROAM & CO was founded in Bangalore by two frequent travellers who couldn't find bags that were both beautiful and bulletproof. We spent 18 months testing materials, visiting factories, and obsessing over every zipper, strap, and seam.

Today we ship to 50+ cities across India, and every single bag still goes through the same rigorous quality checks we started with on day one.
